Team History

The Mets joined Major League Baseball in 1962 to fill the National League void in New York. Their first World Series appearance came in 1969 (the “Miracle Mets”), with another crown following in 1986. Through ups and downs, the orange-and-blue nation stands proud.

Why We Love Them

  • Passionate blue & orange tradition
  • Beloved icons like Tom Seaver & Keith Hernandez
  • Modern stars carrying the torch—Degrom, Lindor, Alonso
  • Never-say-die spirit at Citi Field
